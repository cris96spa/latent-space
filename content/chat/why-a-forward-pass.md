Anyone can list what they've built. A bulleted portfolio tells you what I shipped and
nothing about how I think - and how I think is the actual product.

So the site behaves like the thing I work on: it tokenizes a prompt, runs a forward pass,
and streams the answer, because that says more about me than a grid of logos. It's nerdy,
it's a little unconventional, and it's detailed on purpose. If that's not for you, the back
button is right there.
