The [Smol Training Playbook](https://huggingface.co/spaces/HuggingFaceTB/smol-training-playbook),
by the Hugging Face SmolLM team. A book-length account of what training a strong LLM
actually takes: the ablations, the data fights, the infrastructure, and everything that
broke on the way to SmolLM3's 11T tokens. My paper stack is an infinite
recursion; this one earned a base case.
