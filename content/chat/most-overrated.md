"AI is conscious." "AI will take over the world." "Coding is dead." Let's denoise that.

An LLM is a pile of weights on a GPU. It does probabilistic pattern-matching extremely well,
and that's useful - but there's no consciousness, no understanding, no reasoning
in there. Claiming otherwise isn't visionary, it's just not reading the code.

And the hype buries the useful part: "anyone can ship a product now," sure - right until
your friend asks why `localhost:8000` doesn't open from his phone. The typing was never
the bottleneck. Knowledge is. Optimize that.
